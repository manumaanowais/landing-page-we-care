Safety Measures We Implement

Proper Ventilation

Ensure adequate ventilation in the repair area to prevent the buildup of harmful gases or fumes.

Power Off

Always turn off the power supply to the appliance before starting any repair work to avoid electric shocks.

Use of Insulated Tools

Use insulated tools and equipment to minimize the risk of electrical accidents while working on live circuits.

Protective Gear

Wear appropriate protective gear such as gloves and safety goggles to protect yourself from sharp edges and chemicals.

Secure Workspace

Ensure the workspace is clean, organized, and free from clutter to prevent tripping hazards and accidents.

Proper Handling
